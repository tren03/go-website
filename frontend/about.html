About This Site

Welcome to Artechcles! I built this site to share interesting tech articles that I come across. It's a way for me to keep track of these articles and also share them with others who might find them helpful or fascinating.

Please note that none of the articles posted on this site are written by me. All credit goes to the original authors and sources, and links to the original articles are provided for proper attribution.

Fun fact: This website was written in Go and HTML (because who doesn't love a good combo of simplicity and speed?). But wait, there's more! I'm planning to rewrite the frontend with a fancy framework soon. React, Vue, Svelte... who knows? Stay tuned for that glow-up!
